# Synthesis Checklist

- Did you separate published papers from working papers?
- Did you identify the top-journal anchors first?
- Did you organize by mechanism or design rather than by paper order?
- Did you record identification strategy for the core empirical papers?
- Did you flag where evidence is provisional or frontier-heavy?
- Did you state when only titles or abstracts were available?
- Did you avoid invented citations and unsupported claims?
- Did you end with explicit research gaps or next-step reading suggestions?
